ABOUT JUSTICE UGANDA

Justice Uganda is a non Government Organisation that has been put in place to solve all the necessary cry of the Ugandan citizen with queries associated with unfullfilled justice. This comes at a time when the United Nations Body ranks Uganda as one of the the African countries with high rates of injustices since 2005. We do this through giving free consultation on issues related with all issues that bring about concern in the courts of law and many more.
